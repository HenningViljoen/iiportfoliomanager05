Portfolio management through data science

We use Airtifical Intelligence to maximize your return, minimize your risk, and invest in 40 countries across the globe for your particular risk profile.

For many people, investing outside their home countries, is something they do not know much about, but they would like to be able to put some of their savings in off-shore markets. Not knowing where to put the funds, and what the options are, are often hurdles to investing abroad.

Our goal is to open up the opaqueness of foreign investment, and to do so using Artifial Intelligence (AI). Cutting edge Machine Learning and Data Science techniques can extract value from the large volumes of data from the markets much better than humans can, and in the process AI can maximize return, and minimize risk given the investor's risk appetite.

Data from the last couple of decades indicates that most active management portfolio managers do not beat the S&P500 index when measured over a 10 year period. As a minimum, the index has been doing very well, and also offers a natural risk hedge since many securities are invested in. This argument is extended by our app by investing in off-shore indeces for each country's stock exchange that is included in the hyperspace to be optimized.

The hyperspace to be optimized is characterized by this loss function to be minimized: PortfolioRiskReturnBalance = wTEw - qRw.
